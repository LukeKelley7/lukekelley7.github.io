About Me

I grew up in inland California in the town of Ridgecrest. I graduated from high school there with high honors, and from there decided to continue my journey at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo studying architecture. After studying architecture for 3 years, I decided the major was not right for me, and took a leave of absense and returned home. While at home I worked on at the China Lake Naval Base, mostly doing basic machining and metal working (such as welding). After two years of such work, I returned back to school and finished a degree in economcs, where I succeeded and excelled far above what I expected after architecture.

From here, I am going to work as an intermediate youth leader for a church back home for a few months until I start my position as a Contract Specialist on the China Lake Naval Base.

My interests and hobbies include playing chess and other strategy games, singing and playing saxophone for my local church, and hiking the nearby Sierra Nevadas.
